Study in Bhutan at CST

"Studying at the College of Science and Technology in Bhutan was an unforgettable experience that offered a unique blend of academic excellence, cultural immersion, and adventure. As a foreign student, I found myself warmly welcomed into the close-knit community at CST, where dedicated faculty members provided rigorous education and mentorship in science and technology fields. Beyond the classroom, Bhutan's rich cultural heritage and breathtaking natural landscapes provided endless opportunities for exploration and personal growth. From participating in vibrant festivals to trekking through pristine forests, every day was an adventure filled with new discoveries and experiences. Moreover, CST's focus on sustainability and experiential learning opportunities further enriched my academic journey, equipping me with invaluable skills and insights for the future."


Jasmine
Class of 2021
