    • CST

    The College of Science and Technology (CST) is the first institute in the country to offer undergraduate degree programmes in engineering under the Royal University of Bhutan. The college aspires to be a centre of excellence in the field of science and technology-enriched with GNH values by offering quality programmes that are relevant to the need of the job market both within and outside the country.

      Vision

    The vision of the College of Science and Technology (CST) is to become a center of excellence in science and technology, enriched with Gross National Happiness (GNH) values.

      Mission

    Complementing this vision, CST's mission encompasses offering internationally recognized programs in science and technology, fostering research and innovation to address societal needs, collaborating with stakeholders to provide expert services, and engaging in community outreach initiatives to contribute to the development of a GNH-based society.
